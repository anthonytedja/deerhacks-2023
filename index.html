    frequently asked questions

    DeerHacks is University of Toronto Mississauga's annual hackathon where hackers of all levels are welcome to join us for a weekend of prizes, development, and innovation. Organized by the MCSS Club, we welcome you to join us in our second DeerHacks Hackathon!

    April 28-30, 2023 will be the event time for this year's hackathon. applications will open up on {DATE}. the hackathon will be fully in-person this year and the deadline to sign up will be on {DATE} 11:59pm.

    Participants will be able to attend workshops, competitions, and fun activities to enjoy throughout the hackathon. participants will also have the opportunity to present their final project to a panel of experienced judges and industry veterans, as well as networking with sponsors and peers.

    DeerHacks will enable you to learn something new, network and build connections, get rewarded for your hard work, and eat free food from our caterers! all you need to bring is your laptop (and possibly a sleeping bag to get comfy).
